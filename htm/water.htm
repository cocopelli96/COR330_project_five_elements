  • Taste: Salty

What is water?

Water is the element of rest. There is almost no energy seen in this phase and it depicts the idea of just going with the flow and drifting down the river peacefully. It also explains why the beast of water is often seen to be the tortoise a slow and restful creature. Winter is the season of water because it is a time when the world seems to be the most at rest. The coldest time when animals hibernate and plants seem to stop growing.

Water is born from the metal on which it forms. It can be used to put out the raging flames of fire. Water must flow with the form of the solid earth and it give way to the production of wood.

It might be at this time that you say, "But water over time erodes earth and can reshape the land." This is a true fact and the reason this works is the element cycle. Ironically the cycle can be manipulated in ways to give an element power over other it should not be able to control. This is due to the cyclical nature and that if too much power is put into one phase it can overwhelm another phase. To explain this look at how earth gives its energy to metal which gives its energy to water. Effectively the power of earth eventually becomes the power of water and so earth only controls water when it does not first give its energy to metal. Another view is that water gives way to wood and wood controls the earth. This means that through the production of wood water can control earth.
