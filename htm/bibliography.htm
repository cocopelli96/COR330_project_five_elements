Works Consulted

This page is a list of works consulted to create the information on this website. Throughout the site you will find small numbers as superscripts to different lines of text. These are links which bring you to this page for reference. Each number is based on the location of the source in the list below. So a superscript 2 relates to the source in this list marked as 2.

I chose this method of presentation as it doesn't disrupt the flow of the information in the website. In this way a person who doesn't care about the source of the information can read through the site unencumbered, whereas those who seek more information can proceed to look up the source. In this way the site can work best for a number of different audiences both lay persons and scholarly.
