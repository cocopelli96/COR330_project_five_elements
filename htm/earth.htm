What is earth?

Earth is the true neutral element. In almost any version of the Five Element Theory you can find earth is designate as the median of everything. It is between water and fire, metal and wood, and even yin and yang. Earth is the phase denoting the perfect balance of energy and potential. As Earth is an acting buffer to the other elements it is the only one that can be found to classify things that might not completely fit into the normal order of the elemental cycle. For instance in the seasons earth is often seen as the late summer because it fits in the order of the element cycle. However, earth can represent anytime in between any of the seasons or even no season at all.

Earth is born from fire and within the earth can be found the element of metal. Earth is restricted and controlled by wood and the roots of plants which keep it in place. But earth can shape the flow of water as it runs down mountains or across river banks.

Earth is also the ideal phase in many ways. The Chinese see earth as the center and believe the ancient and perfect Chinese dynasty had been in the an era controlled by the element of earth. This is related to how China means "Middle Kingdom", middle as in the center or the elemental phase of earth.
