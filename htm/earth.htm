What is earth?

Earth is the true neutral element5. In almost any version of the Five Element Theory you can find earth is designate as the median of everything. It is between water and fire, metal and wood, and even yin and yang. Earth is the phase denoting the perfect balance of energy and potential5. As Earth is an acting buffer to the other elements it is the only one that can be found to classify things that might not completely fit into the normal order of the elemental cycle. For instance in the seasons earth is often seen as the late summer because it fits in the order of the element cycle5. However, earth can represent anytime in between any of the seasons or even no season at all5.

Earth is born from fire and within the earth can be found the element of metal. Earth is restricted and controlled by wood and the roots of plants which keep it in place. But earth can shape the flow of water as it runs down mountains or across river banks.

Earth is also the ideal phase in many ways. The Chinese see earth as the center and believe the ancient and perfect Chinese dynasty had been in the an era controlled by the element of earth. This is related to how China means "Middle Kingdom", middle as in the center or the elemental phase of earth. We can also see this in the way that if you were to classify humanity under an element they would be found under earth. This is because the creature characteristic of earth is naked like humans3.

The earth is a powerful element in Chinese culture because the vitality of life comes from the earth. The earth produces bounty both the nutrients for plants and sweets like honey as well as precious metals4. This might also be why the earth is liked with the number 5 which is an important number in China3. It may not be a lucky number per say but it is found quite often throughout the world and is part of the founding idea of the five element theory.
