Fire

Classifying the World

  • Beast: Phoenix or Scarlet Bird
  • Direction: South
  • Color: Red
  • Organ: Heart
  • Season: Summer
  • Taste: Bitter
  • Planet: Mars
  • Creature Characteristic: Feathered

What is fire?

Fire an embodiment of life and death, a duality in its own nature. In the Five Element Theory fire relates to phase with the greatest amount of energy. This makes sense because the element of fire is pure energy giving off both light and heat. It is here that we can also find that fire is powerful for two reasons: the life it gives through light and warmth and the death it brings through the harm of its flames.

Fire follows the element of wood, the fuel that allows the fire to grow and burn. Fire in turn gives birth to the earth which is in the form of ash that remains from the dying fire. Earth cannot be completely consumed by fire and thus is stronger than it. Metal however is vulnerable to the effects of fire because it has the ability to mold the metal into a new form. Water can put out the flames of the fire though.
